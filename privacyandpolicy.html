1. Introduction

Welcome to Authra, a two-factor authentication (2FA) application developed by TriVisionTech. Your privacy and security are fundamental to our design.

This Privacy Policy explains how Authra handles user data.

2. Core Privacy Principles

Authra is built with a privacy-first approach:

  • No user accounts
  • No data collection
  • No internet dependency
  • No data sharing

3. Data Collection

Authra does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

We do not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Usage data
  • Device identifiers
  • Analytics or tracking data

4. Local Data Storage

All authentication tokens and related data are stored exclusively on your device.

This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible by TriVisionTech
  • Is not backed up to external servers

5. Security

Authra follows industry best practices for secure local data handling.

However, users are responsible for maintaining the security of their device. We recommend:

  • Enabling device lock (PIN, password, or biometrics)
  • Keeping your operating system updated
  • Preventing unauthorized access to your device

6. Third-Party Services

Authra does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics providers
  • No advertising networks
  • No external APIs

7. Data Retention

Since no data is collected or transmitted, Authra does not retain any user data.

All information remains on your device until you manually delete it or uninstall the application.

8. User Control

You have full control over your data at all times.

You can remove all stored information by:

  • Deleting accounts within the app
  • Clearing app data
  • Uninstalling the application

9. Children's Privacy

Authra does not knowingly collect any information from users of any age, including children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
